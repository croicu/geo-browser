# BRIEF.md

Google Maps knows the fastest route.
It doesn’t know the best one.

RioneSanità shows you where city life actually is — before you walk into the wrong street.

**And while you’re walking**, glance at your location to decide: left street or right street? Lively or dump?

Open source. Offline first. No ads. No sponsored pins. No blue line to follow.
